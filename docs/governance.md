# Governance model

The original project is a multi-role research workflow. This public reference keeps the governance idea while avoiding private market history, real schedules, and personal preferences.

## Role separation

| Role | Responsibility |
|---|---|
| Macro research | Records broad economic context and uncertainty. |
| Event research | Identifies material events and their possible market relevance. |
| Sector research | Challenges or supports sector-specific claims. |
| Source audit | Checks provenance, date, and competing interpretations. |
| Synthesis | Converts evidence into a concise decision proposal. |
| Policy audit | Applies the default-to-HOLD rule and checks mechanical constraints. |

## Decision protocol

1. Evidence must be dated and attributable.
2. Missing or contradictory information defaults to **HOLD**.
3. A proposed purchase must pass the configured allow-list, confidence, and position-limit gates.
4. The deterministic ledger records only paper activity.
5. A decision audit records what would change the conclusion.

This is governance documentation, not a financial model or a claim that prompt-level rules alone can prevent bad decisions.
