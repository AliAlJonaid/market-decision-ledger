# Limitations

- Educational systems-design prototype only; it does not connect to brokers, execute trades, or provide financial advice.
- All committed examples are synthetic and do not represent investment performance.
- The deterministic ledger can enforce simple mechanical constraints, but it cannot verify the truth or completeness of research inputs.
- An allow-list in this repository is illustrative and is not a Sharia certification, security screening, or recommendation.
- No backtesting, transaction costs, taxes, slippage, foreign exchange, or market-liquidity model is included.
- The CSV event log is append-only by application convention, not immutable or tamper-evident; the recovery journal assumes a single writer.
- A short paper record would not establish an investment edge even if it were favorable.

The useful takeaway is the separation of evidence gathering, policy gates, deterministic state, and human review—not a promise of returns.
